The Hedgehog is about 20 centimeters long and is a large species.
Spines are raised on the body, and overall, they are strong and have dark patterns shaped like horizontal lines.
When faced with a natural enemy, the four legs are gathered on the belly and rounded like a ball to defend themselves with thorns on their backs.
The ears are short, the snout is pointed, and the toenails are well developed.
From November, she enters hibernation and thorns, gives birth to three to seven babies per stomach, and the pregnancy period is about one month.
