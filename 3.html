Siamese

Siamese cats are a breed of cats.
It is originally a short hair species, but it also has a long hair called baliniz and has a slim body and short, fine hair.
The body color is generally grayish white, with dark ears, tails, snouts, and brown legs at the end of the body.
Overall, Siam cats are covered in short, grayish-white or tan hair, and have darker fur than base hair on their tails.
At birth, the whole body is white, but by the age of one, the black part becomes clearer, and gradually dark colors appear.
The ambient temperature and body temperature affect the color, and the warmer the ambient temperature, the more distinct the color of the fur.
The fur color is varied, but the eyes are one vivid sapphire blue color.
The ears are big and the hind legs are longer than the front legs. The tail is long and narrow, so it stands out and gives an elegant feeling.
He likes to be loved and active in expressing affection, such as rubbing against his owner's body.
